Our web application “Future of Programming Languages” is designed to explore and present insights about how programming languages are evolving. 
The purpose of this application is to provide students, developers, and researchers with a clear understanding of upcoming trends, emerging languages, and the impact of technologies like artificial intelligence, low-code platforms, and automation on programming. 
By highlighting future possibilities, this platform helps learners prepare for industry demands and guides professionals in choosing the right skills and languages to stay relevant in the fast-changing world of software development.
